Welcome to MONETA

Your all-in-one solution for Point of Sale, Inventory Management, and Financial Reporting.

A Seamless Workflow

From product creation to final sale, MONETA streamlines every step.

1. Create & Organize

Define master products and group them into strategic sales catalogues.

2. Procure & Stock

Manage suppliers and record purchase invoices to automatically update inventory levels.

3. Sell & Distribute

Handle direct POS sales and manage bulk consignment checkouts for your teams.

4. Analyze & Grow

Track payments, manage finances, and generate powerful reports to drive growth.

Explore the Modules

A complete suite of tools to run your business efficiently.

Product Management

Centrally create, categorize, and manage your master product catalog, from unit costs to selling prices.

Sales Catalogue

Design curated catalogues for different seasons and events, defining which products are available for sale and at what price.

Purchase Management

Streamline procurement by creating purchase invoices from suppliers, which automatically updates your stock levels upon receipt.

Sales Management

Run your daily sales operations for multiple storefronts like 'Church Store' and 'Tasty Treats' through a fast and intuitive POS interface.

Consignment Management

Empower your teams by allowing them to check out products in bulk, manage their own sales, and report back on activity and payments.

Supplier Management

Maintain a comprehensive database of your suppliers, including contact details and credit terms, to streamline your supply chain.

Payment Management

A central hub for Finance and Admins to manage, verify, and track all inbound and outbound payments across the entire system.

Admin Controls

Manage core application settings, user roles, permissions, and master data like categories, payment modes, and sales seasons.

Reporting & Analytics

Access a powerful suite of reports, from high-level executive dashboards to detailed transaction analysis, to make data-driven decisions.
